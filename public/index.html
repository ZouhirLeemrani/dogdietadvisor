About DogDietAdvisor

Your dog's nutrition, personalized.

DogDietAdvisor was built with one simple belief: every dog deserves a nutrition plan as unique as they are. A Golden Retriever puppy has completely different dietary needs than a senior Labrador with joint issues — yet most dog owners receive the same generic advice.

We built DogDietAdvisor to change that. By combining your dog's specific profile — breed, age, weight, activity level, health concerns, and budget — with advanced nutritional analysis, we generate a personalized plan that actually fits your dog's life.

What We Do

DogDietAdvisor analyzes your dog's unique profile and generates:

  • A personalized daily nutrition and calorie plan
  • Food brand recommendations matched to your dog's needs and your budget
  • A breed-specific lifestyle and exercise guide
  • A clear list of foods and risks to avoid

Our Approach

We believe good nutrition advice should be accessible to every dog owner — not just those who can afford a veterinary nutritionist. DogDietAdvisor is free to use, with a Pro option for unlimited plans and advanced features.

Important Disclaimer

DogDietAdvisor provides general wellness guidance only. Our recommendations are not a substitute for professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for medical concerns about your dog.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At DogDietAdvisor, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you use DogDietAdvisor, we collect:

  • Dog profile data you enter (breed, age, weight, health concerns) — used solely to generate your nutrition plan
  • Usage data — how many plans you've generated, stored locally in your browser
  • Email address — only if you create an account (optional)

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your dog's personalized nutrition plan
  • To enforce the free plan limit (stored in your browser, not our servers)
  • To improve our service based on usage patterns

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not store your dog's profile data on our servers beyond the time needed to generate your plan

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember how many free plans you've used. No tracking cookies are used on this site.

Third-Party Services

DogDietAdvisor uses the following third-party services:
